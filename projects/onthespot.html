On The Spot!

Unreal Engine 5 - Blender - C++ - Adobe Illustrator

• Context

A third-person video game created during the Game Maker's Toolkit Game Jam. LincoLn and I were responsible for the design and implementation which took place over a span of two days from July 15th to 17th, 2022. The game is a 3D platformer in which the player must reach the end of each of the six levels by jumping on platforms that match the number on the side of the dice with the number on the platform. The theme of the Game Jam was 'Roll of the Dice'.

• Concept

A 3D puzzle videogame where the player uses a dice as the main character and must reach the end of each level by walking only on platforms with the same number as the side of the dice that lands on it would require the player to carefully plan their movements and find the best path through each level. The player would need to navigate around obstacles and avoid falling off of the platforms, all while trying to reach the end of the level as efficiently as possible. The game would likely involve a combination of strategy, planning, and quick reflexes as the player navigates the dice through the 3D environments. Overall, the goal of the game would be to use the dice's unique movement abilities to overcome challenges and reach the end of each level.

• Future Improvements

The playable demo is a proof of concept of the game. The game is not finished and there are many things that can be improved. The main improvements that we are currently working on are:

  • Increase the enjoyment of the game by adding personal abilities that the player can use to overcome obstacles or to shorten the path.
  • Adding an energy bar that the player can use to perform special actions and also to let him walk in wrong platforms.
  • Add environmental events that can affect the player's path.
